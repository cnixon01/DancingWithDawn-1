ABOUT DAWN RANDALL

Dawn’s dancing career began in the kitchen with her mother teaching her time steps long before ever setting foot in a studio. After becoming an assistant dance instructor as a teenager, she knew it would be her lifelong goal to inspire children through the arts. Dawn’s years of teaching experience have led her to become the director of a successful dance program through the Dade County Public School system, reaching out to over 200 families every year. Her students have won awards at competitions, earned scholarships to prestigious dance programs, and have gone on to have successful careers in the arts. Her classes foster an “I Can Do It” attitude where all learners are encouraged for their efforts.

"I started my lifelong career of dance at the age of 4 with Miss Dawn and now she teaches my own daughter. She instilled a deeply rooted passion and appreciation for the arts which led me to dance with professional companies and now teach dance as a college professor."

Jessica Mayhew-Borrero, MFA Florida School of the Arts
