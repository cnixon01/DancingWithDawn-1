Fun For Boys & Girls

Classes are designed with the specific needs of each age group in mind. Children can begin enjoying music and movement classes at ages 2 1/2.

Great Music

Fun engaging music to encourage children to find many unique ways to move and groove. Positive messages to promote cooperation and life skills.

Playful Props

Kids love using all kinds of props such as bananas, bean bags, ribbon wands, teddy bears, "magic" strings, egg shakers, flags, hoops, scarves as well as any other fun themes I can dream up!

About Dawn

I’ve been teaching children all my life. Being a child's first teacher in dance and movement is my greatest blessing. I strive to foster an "I Can Do It" atmosphere for all learners. Sharing the joy of music and movement helps me to teach children the important lesson to love themselves and others.
