How much do you charge for your teaching services?

I will be delighted to contract directly with your school to provide instruction for all your classes, or I can work directly with the parents who choose dance and theater classes for their children. You will find my rates competitive and affordable.

Where do you hold your lessons?

Classes are held at preschools, churches and child care care centers in the Gainesville Florida area.

What is your teaching experience?

I began teaching little ones when my dance teacher asked me to be her assistant while still in high school. This expereince has led me to a life-long love of working with children. I was the director of a dance program for Dade County Public Schools in Miami, Florida for 5 years prior to opening my own dance studio. After Hurricane Andrew I closed my studio and began offering traveling dance/gymnastics/physical education/dramatics classes directly to schools and churches. I love visiting each site on a weekly basis and developing relationships with the staff and children.

Is special dance attire required?

Children can enjoy dance and movement classes in their school clothes. I do recommend girls wear shorts underneath dresses. It is necessary to wear tap shoes for tap class and ballet slippers for ballet. However, it is just a simple change of shoes that is required for those two classes.

Are you Insured?

Yes, I am licensed and insured.

Do you offer a free trial class?

Yes, I will bring in music and props and offer a free 20 minute class in your age range(s) and class styles of your choice.
